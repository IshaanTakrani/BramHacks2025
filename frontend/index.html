How it Works

Local fusion of satellite, weather, terrain, and community inputs to calculate a single HZ Index with plain-language guidance.

Detect & Predict

Ingest VIIRS/MODIS hotspots, Sentinel bands, GOES imagery plus weather to forecast hazard probability in the next 6–24h.

Score What Matters

A single HZ Index (0–10) communicates combined risk: Safe, Moderate, High, or Extreme.

Act Early

Adaptive alerts by distance, trajectory, elevation, and access with shelters & routes attached.
